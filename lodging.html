Other Places to Stay

Other Hotels

Waikiki is the tourist district where you'll find the Holiday Inn Express as well as many other hotels and amenities. If you're looking for something far-flung, try Disney Aulani at Ko Olina, or Ritz-Carlton Turtle Bay. But watch out, it'll cost you driving time and money. Click each card to learn more about the area.

Waikiki

Waikiki has tons of hotels, including recognizable names like Hilton, Mariott, and Aston. There are also boutique hotels offering a more unique stay. All hotels are walking distance to the beach, and there are shops and markets everywhere. Downsides are crowds and traffic.

Ko Olina

Located on the west side of the island, Ko Olina hotels include Disney Aulani and other luxury hotels. You'll have stunning sunset views and safe lagoons for snorkeling. The downside is they're pricey, and a far drive from just about everything.

List of Ko Olina hotels

Turtle Bay

Turtle Bay offers the Ritz-Carlton and other luxurious options far away from the city. You'll see pristine beaches and wildlife. It is located at the far northern tip of the island, and so it's quite a drive from everything.
