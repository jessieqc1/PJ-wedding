Other Hotels

Waikiki is where you'll find the Holiday Inn Express as well as the bulk of hotels and amenities. If you're looking for something far-flung, try Disney Aulani at Ko Olina, or Ritz-Carlton Turtle Bay. Airbnbs are also available, but are limited to these same zoned areas. Click each card below to learn more.

Waikiki

Waikiki hotels include recognizable names like Hilton, Mariott, and Aston. There are also unique boutique hotels. All are walking distance to the beach, and there are shops, markets, and restaurants everywhere. Downsides are crowds and traffic.

About Waikiki

Ko Olina

Located on the west side of the island, Ko Olina hotels include Disney Aulani and other luxury hotels. Perks include sunset views and nearby lagoons for snorkeling. The cons are the price and far drive from any amenities or other tourist attractions.

List of Ko Olina hotels

Turtle Bay

Turtle Bay offers the Ritz-Carlton and other luxurious options far, far away from crowds. You'll see pristine beaches and wildlife. Located at the isolated northern tip of the island, it is a long drive to the nearest town.
