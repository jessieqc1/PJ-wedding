Transport

Getting Around

The easiest way to get around is by rental car, but if you don't want to drive, book a private tour van or charter bus to some popular tourist spots. If you really want to live local, ride on the public TheBus system through town or even the far corners of the island. Just note that TheBus does not allow large luggage on board.
